# Vision

## Purpose

The goal of this project is to automate the job search process using AI agents.

## Problem

Searching for a job online takes a lot of time. People often need to visit many job websites, search for new openings, and filter them by role, location, experience level, and other requirements.

This process becomes even more difficult when someone is unemployed and needs to find a job quickly. In addition, many hiring processes take weeks or months, and not every application receives a response.

## Solution

This project automates the job search process by using AI agents to collect, filter, and organize job opportunities based on the user's profile and preferences.

Instead of spending hours searching through different websites, users receive a list of relevant job opportunities that match their skills and requirements. This allows them to focus on the most valuable part of the process: preparing and submitting high-quality applications.

## Vision

The long-term vision is to build an intelligent assistant that continuously searches for new opportunities, helps users discover jobs they might not find on their own, and makes the job search process faster, easier, and more efficient.
